Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

What is included in the $70 ticket?
Your admission includes the four-hour Avalon Yacht cruise, food, DJ music, dancing, and access to the indoor and open-air deck areas. Drinks are not included. Alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks may be purchased onboard from the boat.
When should I arrive?
Plan to arrive by 11:15 AM. Boarding is expected to begin around 11:30 AM, and the yacht departs promptly at 12:00 PM.
How is my ticket confirmed?
Pay through the listed Zelle or Cash App option and send your name and payment confirmation to the Young Generation Council member assisting you.
Where does the cruise depart?
Skyport Marina, 2430 FDR Drive, New York, NY 10010, on Manhattan's East Side.
Will I have enough time before the Inaugural Ball?
Yes. The cruise is scheduled to return at 4:00 PM, allowing several hours to prepare for the evening event.
