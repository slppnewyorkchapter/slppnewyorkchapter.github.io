Executive Inauguration & Ball

Antun's

A Queens Village landmark since 1955, Antun's has long been a favored catering hall for Sierra Leonean and broader West African community celebrations across New York City. Its ballroom, full-service bar, and dedicated event staff make it a fitting home for the Chapter's swearing-in and inaugural celebration.

Frequently Asked Questions

Good to Know

Dress Code+

Meet & Greet is smart casual. The Executive Inaugural Ball is Black Tie or African Formal. Sunday Brunch is smart casual.

Airport+

JFK and LaGuardia (LGA) are both within reach of the hotel corridor and venue. JFK is closest to the room block hotels.

Transportation+

Both hotels offer complimentary airport shuttle service. Rideshare and taxis are readily available for travel to Antun's and around the city.

Hotels+

Three hotel options are available near JFK. See the Hotels section above for rates, deadlines, and booking details.

Parking+

Antun's offers on-site parking. Parking at the Hampton Inn is valet-only at $50/night; self-parking is not available there.

Accessibility+

Antun's is accessible. If you require specific accommodations, please contact the Executive Committee in advance.

Weather+

Early September in New York is typically mild, ranging from the mid-60s to low-80s °F. Light layers are recommended.

Visa Information+

International guests should confirm entry requirements with the nearest U.S. Embassy or Consulate well ahead of travel. The Chapter can provide an invitation letter on request.
